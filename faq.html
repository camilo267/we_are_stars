FAQs

How do I create an account on We Are Stars?

Creating an account on We Are Stars is quick and easy! Simply click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage and fill out the required information, including your name, email address, and password. Once registered, you can start building your talent profile and exploring the exciting opportunities that await you.

Can I update my talent profile after creating it?

Absolutely! We encourage our members to regularly update their talent profiles to showcase their latest achievements and skills. Simply log in to your account, navigate to your profile, and click on the "Edit Profile" option. You can add new photos, videos, and details about your talents to keep your profile fresh and captivating.

How can I connect with casting professionals through We Are Stars?

We provide a seamless platform for connecting with casting professionals. Once you have created your talent profile, casting professionals can search and discover your profile based on their specific requirements. If they find your profile intriguing, they can reach out to you through our messaging system. It's a great way to showcase your talent and connect with industry experts.

Is We Are Stars only for local opportunities, or can I explore international productions as well?

We Are Stars is not limited by borders. While we are an Australian-based talent management group, we have established partnerships with industry-leading companies globally. This means that as a member of We Are Stars, you have the opportunity to participate in both local and international productions. We strive to open doors to exciting opportunities worldwide, helping you expand your horizons and take your talent to new heights.
